Entity Tags

Some common HTML entities:

  • & - Ampersand (&)
  • < - Less than (<)
  • > - Greater than (>)
  • " - Double Quote (")
  • ' - Single Quote (')
  •   - Non-breaking space
  • © - Copyright symbol (©)
  • ® - Registered trademark symbol (®)
  • € - Euro symbol (€)
  • £ - Pound symbol (£)
  • ¥ - Yen symbol (¥)
  • ¢ - Cent symbol (¢)
  • § - Section symbol (§)
  • ° - Degree symbol (°)
  • ± - Plus-minus symbol (±)
