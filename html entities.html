HTML ENTITIES

1. ©

COPYRIGHT TRADEMARK

© 2025. All Rights Reserved

Example: © 2025 indicates that the content is copyrighted in the year 2025.
2. ®

REGISTERED TRADEMARK

This is a ® company of SHIRDI SAIBABA SANTHAN TRUST.

️Example: BrandName® indicates that "BrandName" is a registered trademark.
3. €

EURO CURRENCY

€ is the symbol of Euro Currency

Example: €100 represents 100 euros.
4. £

POUND CURRENCY

£ is the symbol of Pound Currency

️Example: £50 represents 50 pounds.
5. ¥

YEN CURRENCY

¥ is the symbol of Yen Currency

Example: ¥100 represents 100 yen.
6. §

SECTION SYMBOL

The § symbol is used to reference a particular section of a document.

Example: See § 5 for more details.
7. °

DEGREE SYMBOL

The ° symbol represents degrees in temperature or angles.

Example: 90°C (degrees Celsius) or 45° (degrees angle)
8. ±

PLUS-MINUS SYMBOL

The ± symbol indicates a range of values, such as in measurements.

Example: 50 ± 5 means a value between 45 and 55.
9. µ

MICRO SYMBOL

The µ symbol is used in scientific contexts to denote the micro- prefix (10^-6).

Example: 5 µg (micrograms)
10. ¶

PARAGRAPH SYMBOL

The ¶ symbol is used to denote individual paragraphs in legal and academic texts.

Example: ¶ 1, ¶ 2, ¶ 3
11. ·

MIDDLE DOT SYMBOL

The · symbol is often used as a typographical bullet or to separate items in a list.

Example: Item 1 · Item 2 · Item 3
12. •

BULLET POINT SYMBOL

The • symbol is commonly used to create bullet points in lists.

Example:
  • First item •
  • Second item •
  • Third item •
13. …

HORIZONTAL ELLIPSIS

The … symbol indicates an omission or continuation in text.

Example: "To be continued…"
14. ™

TRADEMARK SYMBOL

The ™ symbol is used to indicate that a word, phrase, or logo is a trademark.

Example: BrandName™ indicates that "BrandName" is a trademark.
15. ⁄

FRACTION SLASH

The ⁄ symbol is used to separate the numerator and denominator in fractions.

Example: 1⁄2 represents one-half.
16. ‾

OVERLINE SYMBOL

The ‾ symbol is used to indicate that a number or variable is to be considered as a whole or to denote a repeating decimal.

Example: ‾123 represents a repeating decimal 0.123123123...
17. ℘

WEIERSTRASS P SYMBOL

The ℘ symbol is used in mathematics to denote the Weierstrass elliptic function.

Example: ℘(z) represents the Weierstrass function evaluated at z.
18. ℑ

IMAGE SYMBOL

The ℑ symbol is used in mathematics to denote the image of a function or mapping.

Example: If f: A → B, then ℑ(f) represents the set of all output values of f.
19. ℜ

REAL NUMBER SYMBOL

The ℜ symbol is used in mathematics to denote the set of real numbers.

Example: ℜ represents all real numbers, including rational and irrational numbers.
20. &

AMPERSAND SYMBOL

The & symbol is used to represent the word "and" in various contexts, including programming and writing.

Example: Rock & Roll represents "Rock and Roll".
