Snake

BRIEF OVERVIEW OF SNAKE

  • Snake is a reptile belonging to the suborder Serpentes.
  • Snakes are found in almost all habitats on Earth.
  • They are carnivorous and primarily feed on small animals.
  • Snakes use their forked tongues to sense their environment.
  • Some snakes are venomous, while others constrict their prey.
  • Snakes shed their skin periodically as they grow.
  • They play a crucial role in controlling pest populations.
  • Snakes have a unique skeletal structure with numerous vertebrae.
  • They can be found on every continent except Antarctica.
  • Snakes have been featured in various cultures and mythologies throughout history.
